# Acceptable Use Policy

## DLMC Video Downloader

**Effective Date:** 2025

---

### What This Tool Is

DLMC Video Downloader is a graphical front-end that packages and simplifies access to two
open-source command-line tools:

- **yt-dlp** — a community-maintained video download utility
- **ffmpeg** — a multimedia processing library

This project does not implement any download or media processing functionality itself.
All downloading and transcoding is performed entirely by those upstream tools.

---

### Permitted Use

You may use this tool to:

- Download content **you own** or have created
- Download content that is **explicitly in the public domain**
- Download content where the rights holder has **granted explicit permission** for download or offline use
- Download content for **personal, non-commercial archival** where permitted by applicable law (e.g. fair use / fair dealing)
- Download content from platforms that **explicitly allow** downloading in their terms of service

---

### Prohibited Use

You must not use this tool to:

- Download, reproduce, or distribute **copyrighted material** without the rights holder's permission
- Circumvent or bypass **digital rights management (DRM)** or technical protection measures
- Harvest or scrape content at scale in violation of a platform's **terms of service**
- Download content for the purpose of **commercial redistribution** without appropriate rights
- Facilitate or enable **copyright infringement** of any kind
